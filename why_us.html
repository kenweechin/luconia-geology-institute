Field Trips

The funded field trips aim to consolidate the classroom-taught concepts by studying outcrop analogues in Pyrenees, Spain. Students will document the observed outcrops and use them as analogues to the Reservoir Engineering module in Reservoir Characterization topic.

Internship Opportunity

An eight weeks internship program to work in one of our affiliated partners: Shell, Tullow Oil, and British Petroleum. They are the leaders in the energy industry. Students stand a chance to apply the knowledge learnt from the course into the practical work. There will be a mentor assigned from the company to guide the student throughout the whole internship period.
